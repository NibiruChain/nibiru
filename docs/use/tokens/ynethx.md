## Understanding ynETHx

![YieldNest Strategies](https://docs.yieldnest.finance/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F2873068466-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252FDyxbBTdzhJqQAdVgDB8k%252Fuploads%252FjEjVGw88KWyRuuA8fM4Q%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3De808b9dc-1235-4595-bc3d-46f7812fc259&width=400&dpr=3&quality=100&sign=9b002c5b&sv=2)

ynETHx is a MAX LRT (Liquidity Restaking Token) optimized to capture staking
rewards and dynamically reallocate capital across yield-generating
opportunities. By combining restaking with modular DeFi strategies and
AI-assisted risk management, it provides sustainable and composable ETH
exposure. Strategies are designed to target 10-15\% APY, with actual yields
fluctuating depending on market conditions and protocol incentives.

## How to Get ynETHx

- **Swap on Oku (Nibiru):**  
  Trade ynETHx on the [ynETHx/WETH pool](https://oku.trade/info/nibiru/pool/0xe7612e14a05613caf06fad766b484b0a6d146c3a).

- **Mint on Ethereum:**  
  Use the [YieldNest App](https://app.yieldnest.finance/) to mint ynETHx directly.

- **Nibiru Bridge App:**  
  Move ynETHx between Nibiru and other supported EVM chains with the [Nibiru Bridge](https://app.nibiru.fi/bridge).
