## How to Get cbBTC

There are several ways to acquire cbBTC on Nibiru:

- **Bridge from Ethereum (or Base):**
  Use the [Nibiru Bridge](https://app.nibiru.fi/bridge) to transfer cbBTC from Ethereum to Nibiru.

- **Swap on Nibiru:**
  cbBTC can be purchased on Oku (Uniswap v3 on Nibiru).
    - [cBBTC/stNIBI market on Oku](https://oku.trade/app/nibiru/pool/0xd66ecd109743868c89d7fc07d783224ec4925c25?isFlipped=true)

- **From Coinbase or Ethereum DEXs:**
  cbBTC is available directly from Coinbase or on Uniswap (Ethereum). Once acquired, it can be bridged to Nibiru using the Nibiru Bridge.
    - [WBTC/cbBTC market on Ethereum Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/explore/pools/ethereum/0xe8f7c89C5eFa061e340f2d2F206EC78FD8f7e124)
    - [USDC/cbBTC market on Ethereum Uniswap](https://app.uniswap.org/explore/pools/ethereum/0x54E58c986818903d2d86dAfE03f5F5E6C2cA6710)
